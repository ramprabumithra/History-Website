THE MODERN ERA

The Modern Era(1500-present era) like the Age of Discovery (spanning the 15th-17th century) and the Enlightenment (in the 18th century). The Age of Discovery was characterized by global exploration, particularly in the New World. The Enlightenment was characterized by an emphasis on reason, individualism, humanism, tolerance, and a skepticism of religious tradition.
The Modern era comprises of multiple iconic figures, their lives having shaped the future of the progeny.
